Quick checks

  • If Game Center leaderboards do not appear, check that you are signed in to Game Center in iOS Settings.
  • If a rewarded ad reward is delayed, check your network connection and reopen the app.
  • If audio is not playing, check the in-app sound settings, Silent Mode, and device volume.
  • You can reset local data or change your callsign from the Harbor Supply Pier menu on the main screen.
